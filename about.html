About Allergen

Discover how Allergen uses cutting-edge AI to help you understand and manage your allergies.

Project Overview

Allergen is an AI-powered tool that analyzes DNA images to detect potential allergies. By examining genetic markers, it provides insights into possible allergic reactions, helping individuals take preventive measures.

How It Works

The system processes an uploaded DNA image, extracting relevant genetic markers associated with allergies. Using deep learning algorithms, it then predicts the likelihood of an allergy and presents results to the user.

Advantages

  • Early detection of potential allergies
  • AI-driven accuracy in analysis
  • Non-invasive and fast results
  • Helps in preventive healthcare decisions
